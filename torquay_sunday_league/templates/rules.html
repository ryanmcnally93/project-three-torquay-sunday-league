Rules

All of the following rules must be followed by all participants of this league. This includes players, managers and any other members if staff or participants.

Failure to abide by these rules can result in match bans, fines, and in more serious cases even expulsion from the league.

Team Registrations

1). All teams must have a minimum of 16 players to enter this competition.

2). All players must be 16 years of age or over.

3). If you have completed filling out your team and player information for this upcoming season, you can either contact our team on 0121 364 8377 for faster application or wait until the deadline arrives.

HOWEVER this is not advised, as if there are any issues with your team or players, it will be too late to fix them.

4). Once the deadline day of 10th August 2023 arrives, no information can be changed, no players can be added, and there are no exceptions to this rule.

5). Any abuse aimed at our team during the application process will be handled swiftly, with the team rejected and, if necessary, authorities contacted.

6). Any rude or profound team names will be rejected.

Transfers

7). All transfers must be complete and our online database updated by 10th August 2023.

8). Players MUST be playing for one team only. Any discovered cases of this will result in the player being rejected from the league and fines for the people involved.

Match Day

9). Teams must arrive on time, with at least 11 players ready to play. If this isn't the case the team short of players will forfeit the match 3-0.

10). Any equipment (nets, cones, balls, etc) provided by the league organisers must be returned once the game is complete. Any missing equipment will result in fines.

11). All team locations MUST provide suitable changing areas for players.

12). Any matches abandoned due to wrongdoing of teams will result in fines to cover the pitch hire cost.

Kits

13). Teams MUST not wear kit colours that clash.

14). Shorts MUST be worn, no trousers are permitted.

15). Players MUST wear the correct footwear with strong, molded studs. Metal studs are not permitted.

16). Shin pads MUST be worn for your own safety.

17). No jewellery is allowed to be worn during gameplay.

Referees

18). Referee decisions are final.

19). Any abuse aimed at our referees will be logged and fines, bans and expulsions may result.

20). Any racial abuse will be dealt with swiftly, with the authorities contacted and members removed from game and league.
